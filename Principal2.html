A very warm welcome to you all. We appreciate your sparing time to visit our website...

Mariakani Secondary School was founded in 1968 a joint harambee day and mixed school. It has since evolved into a five streamed boys boarding school in forms I & II and three streams form III & IV. The school has 752 students and 32 teachers

The school is a powerhouse in co-curricular activities, with our music team reaching the nationals perennially. Our academic performance stands in need of much improvement, so is our discipline. Moreover this is a vibrant institution seeking to reclaim its vision of discovering and developing individual talents through provision of quality education.

In this, our website, we endeavor to inform you of happenings in the school. We shall strive to make it interactive; parents & guardians will download report cards, access termly examination analysis per class and access closing newsletters. All these is towards enhancing our academic performance.

Our enhanced visibility, we pray, will contribute towards achieving our mission of being the preferred center producing well balanced graduates.
